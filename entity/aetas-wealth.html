What Aetas Wealth does

Aetas Wealth provides regulated financial planning advice to private clients, families and business owners across the United Kingdom. The practice operates under the philosophy it calls Lifetime to Legacy: helping clients build, protect and pass on wealth at every stage of life.

Advice is delivered by qualified, named advisers. Clients work with the same adviser from their first conversation throughout the entirety of the relationship. The first conversation is free and carries no obligation.

Specialist areas

  • Pensions and retirement planning
  • Pension inheritance tax planning — including the Finance Act 2026 changes taking effect April 2027
  • Inheritance tax mitigation: annual exemptions, gifting, trusts, Business Relief, Agricultural Property Relief
  • Estate planning and wealth transfer
  • Investment management and portfolio construction
  • Cash flow modelling and lifetime financial planning
  • Later life planning: income sustainability, care funding, estate transition
  • Business owner financial planning: director remuneration, exit planning, succession
  • Protection planning: life cover, critical illness, income protection, business protection
  • Employee financial wellbeing (via Aetas in the Workplace)
