Professional background

Matthew Steiner has spent over two decades in financial services, working across advisory, strategy and commercial development. He founded Aetas Partners in 2022 and established Aetas Wealth as the FCA-regulated financial planning arm of the group.

His focus is on strategy, client proposition design and the commercial relationships that support a distinctive, relationship-led financial planning practice. He is not himself a financial adviser but works closely alongside the advisory team at Aetas Wealth.

Roles and organisations

  • Managing Director - Aetas Wealth: Responsible for strategic direction, proposition and commercial development of the financial planning practice.
  • Business Adviser: The broader advisory and group coordination entity, founded 2022.
  • Aetas in the Workplace: Employee financial wellbeing programme for SME employers, at workplace.aetas-wealth.com.
  • Aetas Ventures: Venture and investment activity within the Aetas Group.
  • Aetas Global: International advisory strand of the Aetas Group.

Professional focus
