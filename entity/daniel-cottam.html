Professional background

Daniel Cottam has over 20 years' experience in personal financial planning, working with individuals and families across a range of financial circumstances. At Aetas Wealth, he advises clients on their overall financial picture — bringing together pensions, investments, protection and estate planning into a coherent, long-term strategy.

Daniel's work covers the breadth of personal financial planning. He takes a whole-of-client view, ensuring that advice on one area of a client's finances is considered alongside its impact on everything else.
