Professional background

Daniel Cottam is a Financial Planner and Pensions Transfer Specialist at Aetas Wealth, helping individuals and families make confident decisions about their financial future. With a strong belief that good financial planning starts with understanding people, he takes the time to learn about each client's circumstances, priorities and long-term goals.

Daniel works with clients through every stage of life, from building and investing their capital, through to protecting their family, planning for retirement and creating a lasting legacy. His specialist knowledge in pension transfers means he is well placed to help clients consolidate and structure their pension arrangements as part of a broader, joined-up financial plan.
