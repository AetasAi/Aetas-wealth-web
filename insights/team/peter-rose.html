Peter is a Chartered Financial Planner and Pensions Specialist with over 50 years' experience advising private clients, families and business owners.

He focuses particularly on the transition into retirement and the years that follow, helping clients structure their affairs to support sustainable income, capital preservation and long-term planning. His work centres on bringing clarity to complex financial arrangements, ensuring that decisions made today continue to support clients and their families over time.

Peter is known for a measured and straightforward approach, grounded in experience, sound judgement and a clear sense of responsibility to the clients he advises.

Outside of his professional work, Peter has a long-standing interest in chess, along with a well-developed appreciation for food, wine and travel. He also enjoys the cinema and theatre and, when time allows, driving his Alfa Romeo Spider, while maintaining a pragmatic distance from DIY.
