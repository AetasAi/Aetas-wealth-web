Why it matters

How life changes what good financial planning looks like

Most people assume financial planning is roughly the same at every age: save more, invest wisely, reduce tax. The principles are consistent, but the priorities shift significantly at each stage.

A 32-year-old and a 58-year-old both need a plan. But the questions they need answered, the risks they face, and the decisions that will make the biggest difference are almost entirely different. Advice that ignores that is advice that does not quite fit.

Aetas Wealth organises everything around life stages because that is how people actually experience money.

1

Your priorities change at each stage

Protection matters most when you have dependants. Growth matters most when you have time. Income matters most when you have stopped working. Good advice reflects that.

2

Your tax position evolves

Pension allowances, ISA strategies, capital gains planning, and inheritance tax all interact differently depending on where you are in life and how your assets are structured.

3

Decisions compound over time

A pension decision made at 35 has 30 years to compound, for better or worse. Understanding what to prioritise at each stage is what separates a plan from a collection of accounts.

4

Major events change everything

Marriage, divorce, business sale, inheritance, serious illness. Each one resets the financial picture. Planning that moves with your life keeps those events from becoming crises.

Our planning process

What working with Aetas Wealth looks like

The same four steps apply regardless of which life stage you are at. The difference is in what we focus on and the decisions we are helping you make.

1

Free conversation

We start with a no-obligation conversation to understand where you are and what you are trying to achieve. No paperwork, no pressure.

2

Full financial review

We look at everything: pensions, investments, protection, tax position, debts, and any upcoming life events that will affect your plan.

3

Your financial plan

A clear, written plan built around your specific situation and stage of life. Cash flow modelling shows you what the plan produces over time.

4

Ongoing advice

Life changes. Your plan should too. We review and adjust as your circumstances evolve, so the plan remains relevant at every stage.
