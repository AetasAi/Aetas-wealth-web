Aetas in the Workplace

Financial pressure is quietly costing your business. We identify exactly where, and fix it. Not a wellbeing initiative. A business performance intervention with a measurable commercial rationale.

The proposition

Most business owners can see the effect. Few can see the cause.

Financial stress doesn't usually announce itself. It shows up as distraction, absenteeism, avoidable turnover, and senior leaders spending time on the wrong problems. The pattern is familiar in most SMEs. The cost is rarely measured.

Aetas in the Workplace is a structured intervention designed specifically for SMEs and professional practices. The first step is always a Workplace Performance Review: a no-cost, no-obligation diagnostic that identifies whether financial pressure is showing up in your business, and what a proportionate response looks like. Where action is needed, a three-pillar programme addresses the root cause. Where it isn't, you come away with a clearer picture than you started with.

The programme

Three structured outcomes

The Workplace Performance Review identifies whether action is needed. Where it is, the programme is built around three pillars, introduced only where each earns its place.

01

Clarity

Understanding what you have, and whether it's working.

A structured review of pensions, benefits and financial support. We identify gaps, inefficiencies and spend that's not performing. Employees start to understand and value what's already in place.

Business outcome: Improved return on existing spend. In one recent engagement, pension charge reductions alone saved £45,000 per year.

02

Confidence

Helping employees feel in control, so they can focus on their work.

Practical financial education for employees, covering budgeting, debt, pensions and planning, in a format that feels achievable rather than overwhelming. One-to-one guidance available where needed.

Business outcome: Reduced financial anxiety. Higher daily focus and engagement. A workforce that's present, not distracted by money worries.

03

Stability

Ongoing support that keeps improving over time.

Continued access to tools, guidance and partner support through the Aetas Collective. Sustaining the improvements made and building financial resilience across your workforce over time.

Business outcome: Improved retention. A demonstrably stronger employer proposition. Reduced pressure on leadership from people-related issues.

Why this is different

Not an EAP. Not a benefits platform.

Most financial wellbeing solutions are designed for large corporate employers, with broad platforms, generic content, and the assumption that employees will engage independently. In a smaller organisation, the stakes are higher and the margin for quiet underperformance is smaller. Our approach reflects that.

01

The Review comes first, always

Nothing is introduced without first understanding your organisation, your workforce and where pressure is actually showing up. No two programmes look the same.

02

Commercially grounded

Every recommendation has a commercial rationale. If the numbers don't justify it, we say so. Fees are presented alongside the savings we identify, so you can see the net position.

03

Managed on your behalf throughout

We coordinate delivery, handle employee communications and oversee implementation. You retain full visibility without managing providers yourself.

04

Built where every employee matters

One disengaged person in a 30-person team is more than three percent of the workforce. The return on addressing it is proportionally large.
