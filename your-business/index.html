Your Business

Financial advice for business owners — where your company and your personal wealth are planned as one.

Book a business review No obligation. FCA-regulated. FRN 458421.

When you own a business, your company and your personal finances are tangled together — a decision about how you take profit affects your tax, your pension, your protection and, one day, what your business is worth when you leave it. Handled separately, those decisions pull against each other.

We advise business owners on the financial planning side of that picture: extracting profit efficiently, building pensions and personal wealth, protecting the business and its owners, and planning a tax-efficient exit — working alongside your accountant and solicitor, not around them.

Your business and your personal future, planned together.

Why it works

A joined-up approach

Most business owners have an accountant, maybe a separate pension adviser and someone else for insurance — and no one looking at how it all fits together.

We connect the business and the personal. How you take profit affects your tax, your pension and the value a buyer eventually sees. Planning those decisions together, with one team who understands the whole picture, is where the real value lies.

And when your people need support, we can open the door to Aetas Performance — so the whole group works to one plan.

1

Business and personal financial planning handled by a single team who see how each decision affects the other.

2

Profit extraction, pensions, protection, exit and estate planning considered together, not in isolation.

3

Working with your advisers

We coordinate with your accountant and solicitor so the advice joins up rather than competing.

4

Alongside you over time

As the business and your circumstances change, the plan adapts with them.
