Frequently asked questions

Who does Aetas Wealth work with?

We work with individuals, families and business owners across the UK who want joined-up financial advice. Our clients range from people in their 30s building long-term financial plans to those approaching or already in retirement, and business owners planning an exit or succession.

What is the minimum investment or wealth level to work with Aetas Wealth?

We do not have a fixed minimum, but our services are best suited to people who have investable assets of £100,000 or more, or who have a clear need for ongoing financial planning such as pension consolidation, estate planning or protection review.

How do I get started with Aetas Wealth?

Start with a no-obligation introductory call. We will talk through your situation and what you are looking to achieve. If there is a good fit, we will arrange a discovery meeting to look at your finances in detail before making any recommendations.

Are your fees fixed or percentage-based?

Both, depending on the service. Initial advice fees are typically fixed. Ongoing management is usually charged as a percentage of assets under management. We set out all fees in writing before any work begins, so there are no surprises.
