Questions we hear most often.

Do I need a financial adviser in my 30s?

If you have a pension, an ISA, or a mortgage, you already have enough at stake to benefit from regulated advice. Most people find that professional advice helps them avoid common mistakes, make the most of tax allowances, and build a plan that fits their life rather than a generic template.

Can a financial adviser help if I already use apps like Monzo or PensionBee?

Yes, and the two work well together. Apps are useful for tracking and saving. A regulated financial adviser does something different: they look at your whole picture, including pensions, investments, protection and tax, and help you build a strategy. You do not need to choose between them.

What does the Finance Act 2026 pension change mean for me?

From April 2027, unspent pension funds will be brought within the scope of inheritance tax for the first time. This affects anyone with a pension they intend to pass on, including younger savers building long-term pension pots. An adviser can model the impact on your situation and help you plan around it.
