Financial Advice with a Human Approach

Financial planning is about far more than money. It is about understanding the people behind it.

Book a free conversation No obligation. FCA-regulated. FRN 458421.

Every client has a different story, different ambitions and different challenges, regardless of their life stage or financial knowledge. Whether you are taking your first financial steps, growing your wealth, building a business, planning for retirement or thinking about the legacy you will leave, your financial plan should reflect your life, not simply your investments.

That is why we take the time to listen before we advise. We explain complex financial matters in plain English, provide honest guidance and help you make informed decisions with confidence, without unnecessary jargon or pressure.

Technology helps us deliver an efficient service, but it never replaces personal relationships. You’ll always deal with experienced advisers who understand your goals and remain alongside you as your life and circumstances evolve.

Our aim is simple: to build long-term relationships based on trust, clarity and advice that genuinely improves your financial future.

Helping you build, protect and transfer wealth with confidence.

Why it matters

Advice shaped around you, not a product

Most financial advice begins with a product and works backwards. We begin with you — your story, your priorities and what you are trying to achieve — and build the plan around that.

It sounds simple, but it changes everything about the advice you receive and the relationship you have with your adviser.

Whatever your circumstances or level of financial knowledge, the plan should fit your life — not the other way around.

1

We listen before we advise

Understanding your circumstances, ambitions and concerns comes first. The plan follows from that, never the other way around.

2

Plain English, always

We explain complex financial matters clearly and honestly, so you can make informed decisions with confidence and without pressure.

3

People, not just technology

Technology helps us work efficiently, but it never replaces the relationship. You’ll always deal with experienced advisers who understand your goals.

4

Alongside you for the long term

As your life and circumstances change, your plan changes with them. We stay with you, adjusting the advice as it evolves.

Our planning process

What working with Aetas Wealth looks like

The same four steps apply whatever your situation. What changes is what we focus on and the decisions we help you make.

1

Free conversation

We start with a no-obligation conversation to understand where you are and what you are trying to achieve. No paperwork, no pressure.

2

Full financial review

We look at everything: pensions, investments, protection, tax position, debts, and any upcoming life events that will affect your plan.

3

Your financial plan

A clear, written plan built around your specific situation and goals. Cash flow modelling shows you what the plan produces over time.

4

Ongoing advice

Life changes. Your plan should too. We review and adjust as your circumstances evolve, so the advice stays relevant as your life evolves.
