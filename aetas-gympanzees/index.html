Our process

A clear path from conversation to confidence

A simple, three-step approach designed around your time, your priorities and your peace of mind.

1

Initial conversation

Around 30 minutes by phone, video or in person. Your adviser listens to your goals, your circumstances and what matters most to you.

2

A clear financial plan

If it makes sense to take things further, your adviser brings together pensions, investments, protection and tax planning into a single, joined-up plan.

3

Ongoing advice

Regular reviews keep your plan aligned with your life as your circumstances change. You are supported at each stage, not left to work it out alone.

About Aetas Wealth
