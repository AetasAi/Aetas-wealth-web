How your first meeting works

STEP 01

Initial conversation

Around 30 minutes by phone, video or in person. Your planner listens to your goals, your circumstances and what matters most to you.

STEP 02

A clear financial plan

If it makes sense to go further, your planner brings pensions, investments, protection and tax planning into a single, joined-up plan.

STEP 03

Ongoing advice

Regular reviews keep your plan aligned with your life as your circumstances change. You are supported at each stage.
