Protection Planning

Safeguard your family and finances with intelligent, tax-efficient protection aligned to long-term goals.

Overview

Protection that fits the life you've built

Life rarely goes exactly to plan. Illness, injury or loss of life can have a profound effect on your family's financial security and on the plans you've worked hard to build. The right protection means those plans can continue, even when circumstances change.

Our advisers review your existing cover and work with you to put intelligent, tax-efficient protection in place. The right policies, structured the right way, can mean the difference between a financial setback and a financial crisis.

How we help

  • Life cover and family protection
  • Critical illness and serious illness cover
  • Income protection
  • Business and key person protection
  • Trust structures for tax efficiency
  • Existing policy review

The Financial Conduct Authority does not regulate Wills, Trusts or Tax advice. The value of your investments can go down as well as up, so you could get back less than you invested. The guidance on this page is subject to the UK regulatory regime and is primarily targeted at consumers based in the UK.
