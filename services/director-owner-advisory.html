Director and Owner Advisory

Bespoke planning for directors and business owners, focused on preservation, growth and your family's long-term objectives.

Overview

Aligning your business with your personal goals

As a director or business owner, your personal and business finances are often closely connected. Decisions you make about pension contributions, dividend strategy, or business protection can have significant long-term implications for both.

Our advisers understand the complexities of running a business and bring together personal and business planning into a single, joined-up strategy. The aim is simple: to help you build long-term wealth efficiently while keeping your business resilient and your family protected.

How we help

  • Personal and business planning combined
  • Pension and remuneration strategy
  • Director protection and key person cover
  • Tax-efficient extraction of profits
  • Exit and succession planning
  • Family wealth and legacy planning
