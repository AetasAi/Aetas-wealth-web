Privacy & Cookies

How we handle your personal information and use cookies on this website.

Who handles your information

Aetas Wealth and Insight Financial Associates

Aetas Wealth is a trading style of Insight Financial Associates Ltd, which is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority under registration number 458421. Personal information you share with Aetas Wealth is handled in line with the Insight Financial Associates privacy policy and the Data Protection Act 2018.

The full Privacy & Cookies Policy that governs our regulated business is published by Insight Financial Associates and applies to Aetas Wealth as a trading style of that firm.

Read the full Privacy & Cookies Policy

Information we hold

When you contact us through this website, by phone or in person, we collect and hold personal information so that we can give you advice and meet our regulatory obligations. This typically includes:

  • Your name, contact details and address
  • Information about your financial circumstances, goals and plans
  • Records of meetings, correspondence and the advice we have given
  • Identification documents, where required for anti-money-laundering checks

We hold this information securely, share it only where necessary to provide our services or meet our regulatory obligations, and retain it for as long as required by law and regulation.

Cookies on this website

This website does not currently use tracking cookies, advertising cookies or third-party analytics. The only data we collect through the site is information you choose to send us through the contact form.

If we add analytics or marketing tools in the future, we will update this page and add a cookie consent banner at that point. Until then, no consent is required to use the site.

Your rights

Under data protection law you have a number of rights, including the right to access the information we hold about you, ask for it to be corrected, or ask for it to be deleted in certain circumstances. The full list and how to exercise each one is set out in the Insight Financial Associates Privacy Policy.

To make a request or ask a question about how your information is handled, contact us at dataprotection@aetas-wealth.com.
