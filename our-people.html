People

Aetas Wealth brings together experienced advisers who work collaboratively to support individuals, families and business owners across the UK.

Our approach focuses on helping clients step back, see the bigger picture, and make confident financial decisions with the right expertise around them.

Chartered Financial Planner

Peter Rose

Peter is a Chartered Financial Planner and Pensions Specialist with over 50 years' experience advising private clients, families and business owners.

He focuses particularly on the transition into retirement and the years that follow, helping clients structure their affairs to support sustainable income, capital preservation and long-term planning across the decumulation years and beyond.

Financial Planner

Daniel Cottam

Daniel helps individuals and families make confident decisions about their financial future, providing clear, personal advice for those looking to grow, protect and ultimately pass on their wealth.

With a strong belief that good financial planning starts with understanding people, he takes the time to learn about each client's circumstances, priorities and long-term goals. Daniel works with clients through every stage of life, from building and investing their capital, through to protecting their family, planning for retirement and creating a lasting legacy.

Business Adviser

Matthew Steiner

Matthew has over 20 years' experience in financial services. He works closely with business owners and leadership teams, helping them step back and bring greater structure and clarity to both their business and personal financial position.

Matthew leads Aetas Performance, helping SMEs recognise and address the commercial impact of financial pressure within their teams. By combining financial wellbeing, employee benefits and strategic partnerships, he helps businesses create healthier, more engaged and more productive workplaces.

Working with others

Specialists when you need them

Financial planning is rarely the whole picture. Some questions sit with a tax adviser, a solicitor or a corporate adviser. Where that's the case, we coordinate with the right specialist rather than trying to be everything ourselves.

If you don't already have those relationships in place, we can introduce trusted professionals we've worked with.

Good advice rarely comes from one person working alone. It comes from the right people having the right conversations at the right time, with you in the room.
