Before you get in touch

What to expect from an initial conversation

Our initial conversations are straightforward and carry no obligation. We want to understand your situation first, before offering any view on whether and how we can help.

Who we typically work with

Individuals, families and business owners who want structured, ongoing financial planning advice. Many of our clients come to us at moments of change, approaching retirement, receiving an inheritance, selling a business, or navigating pension decisions ahead of the 2027 IHT changes.

What happens after you get in touch

One of our advisers will respond within one working day to arrange a short introductory call. There is no pressure and no commitment, we want to make sure we are the right fit before either party proceeds.

Regulated advice

Aetas Wealth trades as a business name of Insight Financial Associates Ltd, which is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FRN 458421). All advice is personal, regulated and fully documented.

Our fees

We operate on a transparent fee basis with no hidden charges. Our initial meeting is complimentary. Full details of our charges are set out in our Costs and Charges document and on our fees page.
