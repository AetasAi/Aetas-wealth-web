What is ROEI?

Your people are your largest investment. Most organisations never measure the return.

Every employer invests in their workforce — in salaries, pensions, benefits, recruitment, training and wellbeing. These commitments are significant. Yet in most organisations, no one is actively measuring whether those investments are generating the greatest possible return in performance, productivity and retention.

Aetas Wealth supports both sides of the equation. For employers, we help structure and optimise the financial investment in your people. For employees, we provide individual financial planning, pension guidance and life stage support — at every stage from joining the workforce to retirement.

The ROEI Framework

Five stages. One repeatable methodology.

Every Aetas Wealth employer engagement follows this framework — from initial Workplace Performance Audit through to ongoing review and improvement.

Stage 1

Measure

Understand how effectively your current investment in people is performing.

  • ✔Employee benefits utilisation
  • ✔Pension engagement levels
  • ✔Financial wellbeing indicators
  • ✔Workforce demographics and life stage
  • ✔Employee communication effectiveness
  • ✔Benefits spend per head

Stage 2

Diagnose

Identify where the gaps are and what they are costing the organisation.

  • ✔Financial stress and presenteeism
  • ✔Poor benefit engagement
  • ✔Avoidable staff turnover
  • ✔Recruitment cost pressure
  • ✔Productivity and focus
  • ✔Retirement and succession planning gaps

Stage 3

Recommend

Practical initiatives with a clear commercial rationale — nothing introduced without one.

  • ✔Financial wellbeing programmes
  • ✔Employee benefits review
  • ✔Pension education and engagement
  • ✔Life stage financial planning
  • ✔One-to-one financial guidance
  • ✔Communication strategy

Stage 4

Implement

Aetas coordinates delivery through trusted partners. Full visibility without managing providers.

  • ✔Managed delivery across all agreed services
  • ✔Employee communications handled
  • ✔Provider coordination and oversight
  • ✔Progress reporting throughout

Stage 5

Measure Again

ROEI is a continuous cycle, not a one-off exercise.

  • ✔Engagement and retention metrics
  • ✔Benefits utilisation rates
  • ✔Employee financial confidence
  • ✔Absence and productivity data
  • ✔Organisational performance review
