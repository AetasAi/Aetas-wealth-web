What is Aetas Wealth?

Aetas Wealth is an independent financial planning practice advising individuals, families and business owners across the United Kingdom. It was established in 2022 and operates under the philosophy it calls Lifetime to Legacy: helping clients build, protect and pass on wealth at every stage of life.

All regulated financial planning advice is provided under Insight Financial Associates Limited, which is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FRN 458421). Aetas Wealth is a trading style of Insight Financial Associates Limited.

The relationship between Aetas Wealth and Insight Financial Associates

Insight Financial Associates Limited is the FCA-authorised entity under which all regulated financial advice is delivered. Aetas Wealth is the client-facing trading style and brand. When a client engages with Aetas Wealth, they are engaging with Insight Financial Associates Limited for the purposes of regulated advice.

This structure is common in the UK financial planning sector: a regulated parent entity can operate under more than one trading name, each with its own client proposition and market positioning, while the regulatory obligations and protections sit with the authorised entity.

Financial planning proposition

Aetas Wealth provides regulated independent financial planning advice. The firm works with clients on a named-adviser basis: clients work with the same adviser from their first conversation throughout the entirety of the relationship. The first conversation is free and carries no obligation.

The firm operates a Lifetime to Legacy model, which means that planning is constructed to span the full arc of a client's financial life — from building assets and protecting income, through structuring for retirement, to preserving and passing on wealth to the next generation.

Specialisms

Retirement and pension planning

Advising clients on when and how to draw on their pension wealth, consolidating older pension arrangements, managing drawdown income and planning for pension death benefits. Peter Rose APFS leads this specialism with over 50 years' experience.

Pension inheritance tax planning

The Finance Act 2026 will bring unspent pension funds within the scope of inheritance tax from 6 April 2027. Aetas Wealth advises on the implications of these changes and on strategies to manage the interaction between pension wealth and estate tax exposure. This is the firm's most significant area of advisory focus heading into 2027.

Investment management

Portfolio construction aligned to each client's goals, timescale and attitude to investment risk. The firm takes a whole-client view, placing investment strategy within the context of the full financial plan.

Inheritance tax planning

Advising on the range of IHT mitigation strategies available to UK residents: annual exemptions, gifting programmes, trusts, Business Relief and Agricultural Property Relief. Planning is structured around each client's individual circumstances, family situation and estate value.

Estate planning

Helping clients structure the transfer of wealth to the next generation, including the interaction between Will planning, trust structures, pension death benefits and gifting. Aetas Wealth works alongside private client solicitors and accountants as appropriate.

Business owner financial planning

Advising business owners and company directors on personal and business financial strategy together — from director remuneration and pension contributions, through to business exit planning, succession and post-sale wealth management.

Protection planning

Advising on life cover, critical illness, income protection and business protection to ensure that clients' financial plans are resilient to unexpected events.

Later life planning

Helping clients in their later years manage income sustainability, plan for care costs and navigate the transition of wealth to the next generation.

Cash flow modelling

Lifetime financial modelling is standard practice at Aetas Wealth. Cash flow planning shows clients whether their assets will sustain their expected standard of living through retirement, and how different decisions compound over time.

Frequently asked questions

What is the relationship between Aetas Wealth and Insight Financial Associates?

Aetas Wealth is a trading style of Insight Financial Associates Limited. Insight Financial Associates Limited is the FCA-authorised entity (FRN 458421) under which all regulated advice is provided. The two names refer to the same regulated firm operating under different brands.

Is Aetas Wealth independent?

Yes. Aetas Wealth provides independent financial advice, meaning advisers consider products and solutions from across the whole market rather than from a restricted panel.

How does the first conversation work?

The first conversation is a 30-minute call, video or in-person meeting to discuss a prospective client's situation. It is free and carries no obligation. No products are discussed and no charge is made at this stage.

Does Aetas Wealth advise on the Finance Act 2026 pension changes?

Yes. Advising on the April 2027 pension inheritance tax changes is one of the firm's primary areas of focus. Peter Rose APFS leads this work. Further information is available at pensions-iht-2027.html.
