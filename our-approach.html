About Aetas Wealth

An independent financial planning practice with offices in Mayfair and Norwich, providing integrated wealth management for individuals, families and business owners across the UK.

Who we are

Personal advice with the strength of a national network

Through our relationship with Insight Financial Associates Limited, a well-established independent advisory firm, Aetas Wealth brings together personal, relationship-led advice with the scale, experience and professional infrastructure of an established FCA-regulated financial advice business.

Our advisers work closely with clients to deliver intelligent, connected advice across all areas of financial planning, including pensions, investments and protection, coordinating with your accountant, solicitor and other professionals where it helps.

At the heart of our approach is a simple belief: wealth management should be personal, transparent and built to last. We take time to understand what truly matters to you, helping you make confident, informed decisions that create lasting prosperity for you, your family and your business.

How we give advice

Regulated, transparent and built around you

Aetas Wealth operates as a trading style of Insight Financial Associates Limited, a company authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority under registration number 458421.

This structure ensures that every client benefits from the strength, security and compliance of a trusted national network, while enjoying the highly personal service of a boutique practice.

You can view the FCA registration for Insight Financial Associates Limited on the FCA Register, or learn more at aetas-wealth.com.
