About Aetas Wealth

The dedicated financial planning arm of Aetas Partners, providing integrated wealth management for individuals, families and business owners across the UK.

Who we are

Personal advice with the strength of a national network

Through our partnership with Insight Financial Associates Ltd, a well-established independent advisory firm, Aetas Wealth combines the personal attention and holistic approach of Aetas Partners with the strength, compliance and resources of a long-standing FCA-regulated business.

Our advisers work closely with clients to deliver intelligent, connected advice across all areas of financial planning, including pensions, investments and protection, supported by the wider Aetas network of professional partners in tax, legal and estate planning.

At the heart of our approach is a simple belief: wealth management should be personal, transparent and built to last. We take time to understand what truly matters to you, helping you make confident, informed decisions that create lasting prosperity for you, your family and your business.

How we give advice

Regulated, transparent and built around you

Aetas Wealth operates as a trading style of Insight Financial Associates Ltd, a company authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority under registration number 458421.

This structure ensures that every client benefits from the strength, security and compliance of a trusted national network, while enjoying the highly personal, holistic service that Aetas Partners is known for.

You can view the FCA registration for Insight Financial Associates Ltd on the FCA Register, or learn more at aetas-wealth.com.
