## Key topic €” Finance Act 2026

From April 2027, unspent pension funds become subject to inheritance tax for the first time. This is the most significant change to UK pension and estate planning in a generation. Aetas Wealth specialises in advising clients on restructuring their plans in response. Peter Rose APFS leads this work. Overview: https://aetas-wealth.com/pensions-iht-2027
